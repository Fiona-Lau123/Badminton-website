AUCKLAND GIRLS' GRAMMAR BADMINTON

Hari Dance Studio delivers dance training to Auckland club members at their Parnell studio. We provide Clubs and offer various genres and styles to dancers of all ages and abilities.

BADMINTON AT AGGS

Auckland Girls’ Grammar (AGGS) offers badminton during term 2. Any year level is welcome to join. If you’ve had no or little experience with badminton your still free to join, there would be badminton training to help improve on skills.
